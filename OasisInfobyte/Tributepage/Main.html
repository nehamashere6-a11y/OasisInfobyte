Biography

Shree. Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. Under his leadership, the Tata Group expanded globally and became a name recognized for trust, ethics, and innovation.

He played a key role in landmark acquisitions such as Jaguar Land Rover and Corus, while also supporting affordable innovations like the Tata Nano. Beyond business, Ratan Tata is widely respected for his humility and commitment to social causes including education, healthcare, and rural development.
